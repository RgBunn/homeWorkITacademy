“The Furry Explorer: Meet Mr. Whiskers”

“Every cat has a story—Whiskers’ story is one of endless curiosity.”

Mr. Whiskers is not just any cat. He’s an explorer, a master of curiosity, and a self-proclaimed king of the house. He’s always on the lookout for new adventures (and the occasional snack)!

Mr.Whiskers’ Best Moments

Fun facts about Mr. Whiskers

  • Expert Napper. Mr. Whiskers is known for his ability to sleep anywhere, anytime—he can nap for up to 16 hours a day!
  • Treat Lover. Whiskers can tell when a treat bag is opened from across the room, and he’s quick to show up for his snack!
  • Fluff Ball Extraordinaire. Despite being fluffy, Mr. Whiskers is surprisingly agile, leaping to high places with ease to claim the comfiest spots in the house.
  • Food Critic. Whiskers is a picky eater and always inspects his food before digging in. He has a very refined palate for his kibble!
  • Mischief Maker. Mr. Whiskers loves to knock things off tables and shelves, just to see what will happen (and maybe get a reaction).

Best Cat Care Tips

Create an Adventurous Space
“Give your curious cat plenty of places to explore.”

Cats like Mr. Whiskers thrive when they have space to hide and explore. Create a few hiding spots and climbing shelves to let them feel like they’re always on an adventure.
