Dog Facts

  • Dogs sense time. Dogs can tell when their owners are coming home and even differentiate between short and long durations.
  • Super sense of smell. A dog’s nose is 40 times stronger than ours, helping them detect diseases and track scents.
  • Dog intelligence. Dogs can understand up to 165 words and commands, and some breeds can learn even more!
  • Moist noses. Dogs’ noses are wet to capture scent particles more efficiently.
  • Dogs dream. Just like us, dogs experience REM sleep and dream about chasing, running, or playing.
